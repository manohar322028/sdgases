What is SD Gases?

SD gases is the major service provider of high pressure aviation grade, medical supplementary oxygen systems for high altitude Himalayan mountaineering.

SD gases deals with all kinds of services with sales and purchase of aviation grade, medical supplementary oxygen system for mountaineering and helicopter use. We specialize in the development, manufacture and post sales service of supplemental oxygen systems for use at high altitude. Sd gases has been in business for over 15 years and, thanks to our customers, has grown to be Nepal's popular refilling and assembling center with involvement of 80% of the total market in mountaineering supplementary oxygen busines.

Why Us?

1. Refilling

We are the only company in Nepal to refill high pressure cylinders with liquid trans fill technology and have been successfully refilling cylinders for mountaineering for 10 years now. Our latest technology helps oxygen bottles to maintain dew point moisture.

2. Sales of O₂ aviation grade, medical supplementary systems

We supply high-quality cylinders, regulators, and facemasks for purchase or rental, making oxygen supplementary systems easy. Our products, including the lightest oxygen cylinder and durable regulator, set a standard recognized for use on Asia's highest peaks. Fast and reliable service.

3. Repair, refill and maintenance of O₂ cylinders

We refill H.P. and L.P. cylinders using a liquid trans fill process. Our specialized services include essential maintenance like nozzle repair/change with genuine Russian spare parts and purging, ensuring reliable and worry-free use of used products for many years on a budget.

4. Rental equipment

Many companies struggle to estimate climbers each season, especially newly established or foreign-based ones facing financial and security challenges. To address this, we offer a solution: renting essential equipment for expeditions, including lightweight cylinders, oxygen masks, regulators, and other necessary items for a supplementary oxygen system.
